  2. Indicator engineRSI, MACD, ADX, ATR, Bollinger Bands, and volume ratio.
  3. Market filterBroad market, technology, semiconductors, and volatility set the backdrop.
  4. Signal scoringOpportunity and risk become explainable contribution items.
  5. Plan outputEmail delivers a pre-trade checklist for human review.
